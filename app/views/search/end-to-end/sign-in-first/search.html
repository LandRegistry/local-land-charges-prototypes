Find land or property for your search

Before you can find a local land charge, first you need to identify a property or area of land. These are known as extent areas.

Use the map to draw the area you would like to search. You can add, edit and delete areas using the map controls.

Not all areas are available on our register yet. If you cannot find an area, contact your local authority for information about local land charges.

Search by postcode or street name

For example, ‘SW1A 2AA’ or ‘High Street’
