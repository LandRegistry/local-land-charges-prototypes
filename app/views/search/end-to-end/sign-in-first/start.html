HM Land Registry hold a register of all local land charges.

Local land charges are financial charges or restrictions on the use of a piece of land or property.

This includes:

  • planning decisions
  • road agreements
  • tree preservation orders
  • conservation areas and listed building notices
  • environmental health notices
To search the register, you will need to create an account.
