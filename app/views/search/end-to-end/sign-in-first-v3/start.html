Search for local land charges from HM Land Registry

Local land charges are restrictions on the use of a property or area of land. This can include:

  • planning decisions
  • road agreements
  • tree preservation orders
  • conservation areas
  • listed buildings
  • environmental health notices

We aim to hold a register of all local land charges across England and Wales.

! Warning Before you start check the area you want to search is on the register
