Search for local land charges on land and property

Use this service to search HM Land Registry’s local land charges register.
We hold a central register for England and Wales.

Local land charges restrict the way you use land and property.
Examples include:

  • planning decisions
  • road agreements
  • tree preservation orders
  • conservation areas
  • listed buildings
  • environmental health notices
Check if an authority has moved to our central register.
